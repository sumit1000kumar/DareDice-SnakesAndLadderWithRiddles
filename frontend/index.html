How to Play DareDice

  1. Roll the 3D dice to move across the board.
  2. If you land on a ladder, climb up!
  3. If you land on a snake, you’ll slide down.
  4. Land on a riddle tile? Solve it to stay — or move back!
  5. Land on a dare tile? Complete it or skip your next turn!
  6. First player to reach the top wins the game!
